Research

I’m a geometer/topologist. What captivates me most about geometry is the interplay among diverse fields of mathematics within a single problem. Consequently, I do not confine myself exclusively to a particular area or tool—I consider myself quite a generalist and aspire to approach problems from multiple perspectives.

At present, my research problems in mind are:

  • Blaschke’s Conjecture in Complex Projective Spaces
  • Signature and Casson Invariant of Milnor Fiber
  • 4-dimensional Einstein Manifolds
